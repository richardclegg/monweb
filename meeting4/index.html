Mathematics of Networks 4th Meeting (22nd July 2005)

This meeting took place at Queen Mary, University of London on 22nd July 2005. Thanks to Raul Mondragon at QMUL for playing host for this meeting.

This meeting was part of a series of meetings on this topic --- there is also a mailing list mainly for information about these meetings.

    Schedule

    11:00-11:30Arrival and coffee
    11:30-12:15Huai Huang (Queen Mary, University of London) "Analysis of Self-similar Traffic using Multiplexer & Demultiplexer Loaded with Heterogeneous ON/OFF Sources" (Abstract) (Slides)
    12:15-1:00 Keith Briggs (BT) "Exponential Random Graphs" (Abstract) (Slides) (Handouts)
    1:00-2:00 Lunch
    2:00-2:45 Varol Kaptan (Imperial College) "The random neural network model and some of its applications" (Abstract) (Slides)
    2:45-3:30 Carla Di Cairano-Gilfedder (BT) "Game Theory Models for Network Security" (Abstract) (Slides)
    3:30-4:00Coffee Break
    4:00-4:45 Uli Harder (Imperial College) "Observations with a Small Network Telescope" (Abstract) (Slides) (Further info)
    4:45-5:30 Richard Law (University of York) "Ecological Networks" (Abstract)
    5:30-6:00 Closing Discussion
    6:00End of conference and drinks at nearby hostelry
