Tim Evans -- Abstract

Scale free networks from self-organisation

I will show how a mechanism using random walks gives a scale-free network. Further any variation of the method will still lead to various scale-free networks. I will also comment on the use of random walks in other network problems. The work is based in part on PRE 72 (2005) 1 cond-mat/040b12
