Mathematics of Networks 5th Meeting (7th April 2006)

The meeting was held at the Saïd Business School, University of Oxford on Friday 7th April. Many thanks to Felix Reed-Tsochas for agreeing to act as host on this occasion.

This meeting is part of a series of meetings on this topic --- there is also a mailing list mainly for information about these meetings. If you are interested in attending please join the mailing list.

Abstracts and talk slides

11:30-12:15Nick Dalton (UCL) -- Spatial networks - localization of structure (Abstract)(Slides)
12:15-1:00 Tim Evans (Imperial College) -- Scale-free networks from self-organisation (Abstract) (Slides)
1:00-2:00 Lunch
2:00-2:45 Alexandru Murgu (BT) -- Information capacity and network coding (Abstract)
2:45-3:30 Serguei Saavedra Sanchez (Oxford University) -- The structure of business: The New York garment industry (Abstract)
3:30-4:00Coffee Break
4:00-4:45 Maziar Nekovee (BT) -- The El Farol bar problem on complex networks (Abstract) (Slides)

5:30-6:00 Closing Discussion
6:00End of conference and drinks at nearby hostelry
