Mathematics of Networks (Jones Abstract)

By considering nested crossings made by a continuous process, we build a tree of crossings that encodes the sample path. From the crossing tree we obtain a test for self-similarity and an estimator for the Hurst index H of a self-similar process.

The estimator, combined with the self-similarity test, is employed to detect and quantify self-similarity in network packet traffic. We also investigate non-stationarity phenomena in network traffic, with respect to time variation of H.
