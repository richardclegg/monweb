Mathematics of Networks 7th Meeting (27th June 2008)

The meeting was held at the Department for Engineering Mathematics, University of Bristol on 27th June 2008. Thanks to Eddie Wilson for playing host. The meeting theme was transport networks.

This meeting was part of a series of meetings on this topic --- there is also a mailing list mainly for information about these meetings. If you are interested in attending please join the mailing list.
